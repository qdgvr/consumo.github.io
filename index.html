Major technology companies have agreed to work with the Pentagon on a classified data initiative, expanding their role in national security after years of public tension over the military use of artificial intelligence.

The agreements mark a new phase in the relationship between Silicon Valley and Washington, where officials are pressing private firms to provide computing systems, models and technical support for sensitive government programs.
